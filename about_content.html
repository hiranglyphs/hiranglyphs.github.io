About Me

I am Hiranmay, a PMRF research scholar at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. I think of myself as an academic wanderer — my interests shift over time, and each new lens adds to how I read the world. My background is in condensed matter physics; my PhD thesis revolves around phases and phase transitions in fermions coupled to ℤN gauge theory. Right now, I'm working on better neural quantum state architectures to study these systems.
